CHAPTER II A Dispatch from the Desk

A quiet quarter ends on the books.

From the Desk of the Editor · 5 April 2026

Five channels went to work in January and, by the end of March, the ledger closed higher than any quarter on record. Return on spend rose three tenths of a point to 4.28×, and the cost of bringing in a customer fell for a third consecutive quarter.

The work was neither loud nor particularly clever. Brand search on Google continued to pay for itself many times over. The creator programme on TikTok held its line. A disciplined rebalance away from under-performing pre-roll freed up spend that found a better home on Meta, where the spring launch carried the paid-social book on its back.

The almanac, as ever, keeps its own counsel. Below, the week-by-week movement of spend and return, and the five campaigns that most rewarded their keep.
